WEEK 1 - DAY 1: LLM FOUNDATIONS
================================

Key Concepts Learned:

1. Tokens
   - Unit of text processed by LLMs
   - ~4 characters = 1 token
   - ~75 words = 100 tokens
   - Models charge per token

2. Context Window
   - Maximum tokens model can process
   - llama-3.3-70b: 128,000 tokens
   - llama-3.1-8b: 128,000 tokens
   - mixtral-8x7b: 32,768 tokens

3. Temperature
   - Controls randomness of output
   - 0.0 = Deterministic (always same)
   - 0.7 = Balanced (recommended)
   - 1.5+ = Creative/Random

4. Transformer Architecture
   - Self-attention mechanism
   - Multi-head attention
   - Positional encoding
   - Feed-forward networks

5. Training vs Inference
   - Training: Learning from data
   - Inference: Generating responses

6. Available Groq Models
   - llama-3.3-70b-versatile: Best quality
   - llama-3.1-8b-instant: Fastest
   - mixtral-8x7b-32768: Balanced

7. Key Takeaways from Karpathy Video
   - LLMs predict next token
   - Training is massive pattern recognition
   - Emergent abilities appear at scale
   - RLHF improves helpfulness

8. Temperature Experiment Results
   - 0.0: Repetitive, deterministic
   - 0.7: Creative but coherent
   - 1.5+: More creative, risk of incoherence
   - 2.0: Highly random, often nonsensical
